Establishment


Khawbung Senior Secondary School is a public institute in Khawbung, Champhai District, Mizoram. It was established in 2022 under Samagra Shiksha (Memo No. B. 11013/19/2019 - DSE(E)), and started from 2023 with Science Stream. The school consists of Grades from 11 to 12. English is the medium of instructions in this school.
